SMS Consent Collection Flow

This page documents how Warrior Tax Pro Elite collects SMS consent from tax clients. Our software is a professional desktop application used by licensed tax preparers. Consent is collected in-person during client intake.

Data Sharing: No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent is not shared with any third parties.

2 Verbal Consent Collection (In-Person)

Consent is collected in-person at our tax preparation office. The licensed tax preparer verbally explains the SMS messaging program to the client before checking the consent boxes in the software. The following script is used:

"As part of our tax preparation service, we can send you text messages to your cell phone. These texts will include secure links to upload your tax documents, and notifications about the status of your tax return, for example, when it's been accepted or rejected by the IRS. You'll typically receive 2 to 10 messages during tax season. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Would you like to receive text message notifications?"

Only after the client verbally agrees does the preparer check the consent checkboxes in the software. If the client declines, the checkboxes remain unchecked and no SMS messages are sent.

Key compliance points:
• All three checkboxes are unchecked by default; the tax preparer manually checks them only after the client gives verbal consent.
• Consent is optional. It is not required to complete the tax return.
• A timestamp is automatically recorded when any checkbox is checked.
• The disclosure text ("Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel.") is displayed at all times next to the checkboxes.
• Links to Privacy Policy and Terms of Service are visible on the consent interface.
• No messages are sent until the preparer actively triggers a notification for that client.

6 Opt-Out & Help

Opt-Out: Clients can reply STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, OPTOUT, or REVOKE to any message to immediately stop receiving texts.

Opt-Out Confirmation: "You have successfully been unsubscribed. You will not receive any more messages from this number. Reply START to resubscribe."

Help: Clients can reply HELP or INFO for assistance.

Help Response: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Msg&Data Rates May Apply."
